Corporate Profile

Initiation & inception of S. J. Consultant & Contractor was in the year 2005 and got registered as a proprietorship firm. The main proprietor of the firm was Mrs. Madhumita Basu. Mr. Dhrubajyoti Basu, husband of Mrs. Madhumita Basu, was entrusted with as the Constituted Attorney of the firm to look after all business of the firm on her behalf (Mrs. Madhumita Basu).

Mr. Dhrubajyoti Basu is a qualified Civil Engineer having wide exposure in various types of critical civil engineering construction sites all over India for a period of 11 (Eleven) years with several construction companies. Mr. Dhrubajyoti Basu, during his 11 (Eleven) years service career, proved himself as a hard-working, innovative, diligent & prompt decision maker for any type of construction problems.

Subsequently, during the period from its inception, the company’s business had a successful and steady growth and earned a good reputation from multifarious construction works. We intended to expand the company’s business in India or elsewhere, either alone or jointly with one or more persons, Government, other bodies, or organizations in the Civil, Structural, Architectural, Interior designing, Consultancy, etc., either through Item Rate Tenders or on Turnkey Basis. With the above conception, the partnership firm was converted to a Private Limited Company with effect from November 2008 under the Companies Act, 1956.

This firm has already completed work worth 25.5 crores of rupees and work in hand is 10 crores of rupees. The firm has already worked on several projects like Pipe laying, Pipe manufacturing, Mechanized earth filling, Building construction, Tower construction, Shoring work by structural members, piling, bridges & culverts, Industrial Structures like Cooling Tower, Turbine Generator House, Railway wagon repairing shops, Overhead water reservoir, Modernization of Railway Stations, Infrastructural facilities for big projects, etc.
